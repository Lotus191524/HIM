Him 🤍

Celebrating Three Years of Us
Him is a digital companion and archival project dedicated to celebrating a three-year relationship milestone. The heart of this project is a curated collection of reasons, memories, and traits—inspired by a physical "Reasons I Love You" jar—transformed into a lasting digital tribute.

🍯 The Concept
The "Jar" is more than just a gift; it’s a time capsule. This project serves as:
A Celebration: Marking 1,095+ days of growth, laughter, and partnership.
A Daily Reminder: A digital space to revisit the specific things that make him unique.

A Living Archive: Documentation of the small details that often get lost in the busyness of life.

✨ Features
The Virtual Jar: A randomized display of "notes" pulled from the collection.

The Timeline: A brief look back at the journey from Year 1 to Year 3.

The "Why": Categorized reasons ranging from personality traits to shared inside jokes.

🛠️ Built With
Love & Patience: The primary frameworks.
Markdown: For structured storytelling.
